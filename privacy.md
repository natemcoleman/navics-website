Privacy Policy
Last Updated: March 14, 2026 

1. Information We Collect (Or Rather, Don't Collect)
This application ("the App") respects your privacy. The App is designed so that your location data and commute history belong strictly to you. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell your location data, routes, or personal information. 2. How Your Data is Handled
All location tracking, route saving, and analytics are performed locally on your device. If you have iCloud enabled, the App utilizes Apple's CloudKit to sync your personal data securely across your own devices. This data is stored in your private iCloud database, which we cannot access, view, or manage.

3. Payment Information
The App is a one-time purchase. All payments are securely processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not have access to your credit card information, billing address, or Apple ID.

We may update this policy periodically. Since we do not collect your contact information, we encourage you to review this page occasionally for updates.
